Key insight
No single region is the obvious choice — the data supports multiple defensible decisions.
W. Europe — highest score, most consistent, leads 5 of 6 dimensions. But declining since 2020.
Latin America — punches above its weight economically. At record high. Strong social fabric.
East Asia — fastest-growing region. Closed a 0.49-point gap with Latin America to just 0.21.
Middle East & N. Africa — greatest need. Wealth does not translate to wellbeing. Freedom is its critical gap.
